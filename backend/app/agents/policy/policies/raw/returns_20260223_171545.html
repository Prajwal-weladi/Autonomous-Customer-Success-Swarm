This guide explains how returns work on Flipkart, so you can easily navigate exchanges, replacements, and refunds.Shopping online is convenient, but sometimes the product you receive may not meet your expectations. This is where the Flipkart Product Returns process makes your life easier.Flipkart Product Returns Process ExplainedThis handy reference guide helps you understand how the Flipkart product returns process works:How the Flipkart Product Returns Process Works:Log in to Flipkart and go to your Orders tab. Tap or click on ‘Return’ to create a request.Select your applicable reason of return. Three options will be available based on your reason:Exchange: Your order will be exchanged for a new identical product of a different size or colorReplace: The product in your order will be replaced with an identical product in case it is damaged (broken or spoiled) or defective (has a functional problem that causes it not to work)Refund: If the product of your choice is unavailable in your preferred size or color or model, or if it is out of stock, you may decide that you want your money back. In this scenario, you may choose ‘Refund’ to have your money returned to you (See 7.)Depending on the kind of product you wish to return, your return request may have to undergo a verification process.Following verification, confirm your decision based on the category of product ordered.Keep all the requisite items necessary for a smooth returns process ready, which includes original packaging, price tags, freebies, accessories, etc.Pickup and delivery of your order will be scheduled and details will be communicated in case of an exchange or replacement.The refund will be initiated and processed if applicable.Your request will be fulfilled according to Flipkart’s returns/replacement guarantee.How Long Does the Flipkart Product Returns Process Take?Once you raise a return request, you will receive an email and an SMS. You can track progress anytime under ‘My Orders’ on your Flipkart account.
